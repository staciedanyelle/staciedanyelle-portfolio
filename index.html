About

The short:

Creative | ENTP | Enneagram 8 | List lover (obviously) | Mom/Wife/Daughter/Sister/Friend - I love my family | Wannabe food critic | Weight-lifter | Non-fiction book lover | Wanderer

The details:

When I was 19, my grandmother gifted me a scrapbook of childhood mementos she'd saved - one was a small doodle that, as a child, I wrote “I wanna be an artist.” I’ve been chasing that dream ever since.

I’m Stacie. I am a multi-disciplinary creative currently thriving in Middle Tennessee.

I’ve always been drawn to creative pursuits and though artists are often characterized as whimsical eccentrics, I don’t work that way and I’ve often felt like a black sheep in this colorful world, but I love it nonetheless. I'm straight-forward, methodical, and self-disciplined with an insatiable curiosity.

On the weekends, I enjoy wandering around with my people until we find a savory snack and a nice glass of red wine. I might be playing a yard game, perfecting my devilled egg recipe, or taking my daughter on a wagon ride.

I love what I do and I'm happy to share a small part of that with you.
